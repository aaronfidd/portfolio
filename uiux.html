My Design Process

  1. Discovery: Understand user needs, business goals, and project requirements.
  2. Research: Analyze competitors, gather insights, and define personas.
  3. Ideation: Sketch wireframes, brainstorm solutions, and map user flows.
  4. Design: Create high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes.
  5. Testing: Validate designs through usability testing and feedback.
  6. Delivery: Prepare assets and collaborate with developers for implementation.
