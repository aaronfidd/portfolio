My Design Process

  1. Discovery: Understand client needs, brand values, and project goals.
  2. Research: Analyze market trends, competitors, and audience.
  3. Concepting: Develop creative concepts and mood boards.
  4. Design: Create visual assets, layouts, and mockups.
  5. Refinement: Iterate based on feedback and finalize designs.
  6. Delivery: Prepare files for print or digital use and ensure brand consistency.
